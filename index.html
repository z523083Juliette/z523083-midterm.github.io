LocalShirt

Introducing LocalShirt, the app transforming how we shop for clothes. By harnessing advanced image recognition, LocalShirt analyzes photos of fast fashion items, then suggests visually similar, eco-friendly alternatives from curated brands.

Users access detailed product information, including materials, pricing, and sustainability practices, aiding informed decisions.

With features like price comparison and wardrobe organization, LocalShirt supports budget-conscious and mindful shopping.

Engage with a community passionate about sustainable fashion and access educational resources to deepen your understanding.

LocalShirt: shop consciously, support sustainability.

Bomberman

In Bomberman, you control a character who navigates through a maze-like grid.

The objective is to strategically place bombs to destroy obstacles and enemies while avoiding getting caught in the bomb blasts yourself.

The goal varies depending on the mode you're playing, but usually involves defeating enemies or reaching a specific destination. Power-ups can enhance your abilities, like increasing bomb range or speed. Be careful though, because one wrong move can lead to blowing yourself up!

Gomoku

Gomoku is a strategy board game similar to Tic-Tac-Toe, played on a grid where players try to get five of their pieces in a row.

The AI analyzes the game board, predicts opponent moves, and selects its own moves to maximize its chances of winning. It typically employs algorithms like minimax, alpha-beta pruning, or neural networks to make strategic decisions.
